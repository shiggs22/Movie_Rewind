Movie of the Month: Dumb and Dumber

In light of the now in progress movie Dumb and Dumber Too, staring Jim Carrey as Lloyd Christmas and Jeff Daniels as Harry Dunne. The duo are back and we can't be more excited! Since the first movie came out back in 1994 we the people were waiting to see and hoping for the two to come back together for another take on the Dumb and Dumber franchise. We were let down when the movie Dumb and Dumberer came out; it just seemed to be a slap in the faces of the people who loved the first movie. The take on the this prequel When Harry meet Lloyd was such an awful movie that most people couldn't watch it for more than 30 minutes! We understand that Harry and Lloyd are "dumb" but come on, this was just down right stupid of a movie.So after that massive fail, we all wanted Jim Carrey and Jeff Daniels back together, on the big screen, playing the characters once again so we can get a laugh that we all remembered from the past.So, the Farelly Brothers answered our prayers and got the team together to make a true second movie to the late Dumb and Dumber!
